Frequently Asked Questions

What are the system requirements? +

OS: Windows 10/11, macOS, Linux

RAM: 4GB minimum

Storage: 500MB free space

Mappy is built with Electron and runs on most modern operating systems.

What file formats are supported? +

Mappy accepts CSV and XLSX files for data import and can export to CSV or JSON formats. Your data should have courses as rows, competencies as columns, and justification text in cells.

How do I edit justification text? +

In Single Course View, select a course from the dropdown to see all its competencies. Click on a justification cell to edit the content directly in the application. After editing, click "Save" to preserve your changes.

Is internet access required? +

No, Mappy works completely offline. Internet access is only needed for downloading updates or accessing online documentation.

How can I contribute to Mappy? +

You can contribute to Mappy Intelligence by reporting issues, suggesting features, or providing feedback through our Discord community.
